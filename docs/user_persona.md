# User Persona

## Persona 1: Recent Retiree

- Age: 56-65
- Situation: recently left full-time work
- Pain point: the morning feels empty without structure
- Need: one easy and non-intimidating action to start the day

## Persona 2: Semi-retired Explorer

- Age: 50-62
- Situation: flexible schedule, exploring new routines
- Pain point: too many choices, low follow-through
- Need: lightweight guidance with some local relevance

## Persona 3: Relocated Older Adult

- Age: 60-70
- Situation: moved to a new city to live near family
- Pain point: unfamiliar surroundings and weak local social ties
- Need: nearby places and low-pressure community activities

## Shared Traits

- prefers low cognitive load
- benefits from specific, concrete suggestions
- often responds better to "start here" than to complex planning
- may enjoy routines, but does not want to feel controlled
